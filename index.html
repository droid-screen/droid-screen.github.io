Frequently asked questions

Does DroidScreen work on Windows and Mac?

Yes, our desktop app supports Windows and Mac, and you can also use DroidScreen in a modern web browser.

Do I need to enable USB debugging?

Depending on your use case, some features may require enabling developer options and USB debugging on your Android device.

Can I type and control my phone with mouse and keyboard?

Yes, DroidScreen enables keyboard typing and mouse control for a smooth desktop-like experience.
